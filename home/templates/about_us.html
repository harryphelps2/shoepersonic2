About us

What we do

Shoepersonic is a website dedicated to trying out running shoes. Every season we'll concentrate on a particular type of shoe. We'll buy a selection of models, run in them and tell you what we think, so you can decide which shoes are best for you. All the shoes we test out are available to buy through our site.

Our Reviews

To make our reviews, we have a decent run in the shoe and then talk about how it feels to run in. You can probably tell it's completely unscripted. If any feature makes a difference to our experience, we'll mention it. If it doesn't we won't. For each shoe we test, we think about the features that make it better for different types of runners.

Who are we?

Harry, Founder

I love running. I've been running since school, and race all distances from 5ks up to Marathons, cross country in the winter and road racing in the summer. I work in London in an office. Like many runners I avoid the tube and run to and from work and as much as I can during the week. Then at the weekend I do a Park run and a long Sunday run in the countryside. I've always found buying running shoes on the internet very convenient but also stressful. Descriptions are always full of marketing jargon and never talk about what they are actually like to run in. That's why I set up this site. So that when you come to buy a running shoe, you understand which features of each shoe will suit you and your running style, and can get the best shoe for you.

Running Clubs

Running clubs are the foundation of the running community in the UK. Every single weekend, when the rest of the country is in bed, there is an army of people (usually volunteers) opening the track, laying out cones or tying tape to trees.

When we set up Shoepersonic, we wanted to show our appreciation for these people and we do that by donating a percentage of our profits to them. When you buy shoes from us you'll be asked to type in your running club. At the end of the year, we then donate 5% of the profits from that sale to that club. Just our way of saying thank you.
